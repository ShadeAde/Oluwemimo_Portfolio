What i'm doing

  • Mailchimp

    Mailchimp is a user-friendly interface, its comprehensive suite of features includes list management, segmentation, automation, and analytics, allowing users to tailor their messages and reach the right recipients at the right time.

  • Klaviyo

    Klaviyo stands out for its emphasis on data-driven marketing. It caters primarily to e-commerce businesses. Through its seamless integration with e-commerce platforms, it enables businesses to harness customer data to send relevant and timely emails, enhancing customer engagement and driving sales. .

  • Drip

    Drip is geared towards e-commerce businesses and marketers. It offers a comprehensive suite of tools for creating personalized and automated email campaigns. It's segmentation and tagging capabilities further enhance the precision of campaigns, while its robust analytics provide valuable insights into email performance and customer interaction.

  • Hubspot

    HubSpot is renowned for its all-in-one marketing. It offers a suite of tools that seamlessly integrates email campaigns with CRM and marketing automation which empowers users to create highly personalized emails, automating delivery based on customer behaviors and interactions. .
